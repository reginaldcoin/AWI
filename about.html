About Us

Empowering mental health practitioners across Asia

Our Mission

At Asia Wellbeing Institute, we are dedicated to enhancing mental health care across Asia by providing comprehensive, culturally-sensitive training programs for mental health practitioners. Our goal is to bridge the gap between Western psychological practices and Asian cultural contexts, empowering professionals to deliver effective, compassionate care.

Our Vision

We envision a future where mental health care in Asia is accessible, culturally appropriate, and of the highest quality. By nurturing a community of skilled practitioners, we aim to contribute to the overall wellbeing of individuals and communities across the diverse landscape of Asia.

Our Values

  • Cultural Sensitivity
  • Evidence-Based Practice
  • Continuous Learning
  • Collaboration
  • Ethical Practice
