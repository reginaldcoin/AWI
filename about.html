About Us

Empowering mental health practitioners across Asia

Our Mission

At Asia Wellbeing Institute (AWI), we are committed to empowering individuals across Asia to heal and build resilience through accessible, affordable, and innovative health programs. Our mission is to enhance and advance wellness for all people in the region by providing high-quality training and support to mental health professionals.

Our Vision

We envision a future where mental health care across Asia is accessible, culturally appropriate, and of the highest quality. By nurturing a community of skilled practitioners and offering a range of specialized training programs, we aim to contribute to the overall wellbeing of individuals and communities throughout the diverse landscape of Asia.

Our Values

  • Empowerment
  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • Cultural Sensitivity
  • Evidence-Based Practice

Key Services

AWI plans to offer a range of specialized training programs including Drawing & Talking Therapy, Suicide Awareness, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, and ongoing consultation and supervision for practitioners. We will also conduct public workshops and customized corporate wellness programs to enhance employee wellbeing.
